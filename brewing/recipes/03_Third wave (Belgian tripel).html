Notes

“I have used both yeasts [T-58 and S-33] for triples and they will both work fine. T-58 is a little more spicy than the S-33. Rehydrate 2 packs for a 5gal batch over 1.075 up to 1.085. The T-58 clears out better for me and it’s cheaper. Start fermentation at about 65F and after a few days when it just begins to slow ramp up to 70F. You should get 75% to 80% attenuation.”
